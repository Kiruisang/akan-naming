AKAN NAMING

This website will give you a name, more specifically a first name according to the day of the week you were born.

The Akans are a meta-ethnicity who primarily live in Ghana and Ivory Coast with a population of around 20 million people who share a lot of common traits. Some of these shared traits include their naming system where the first name of the children depends on the day of the week they were born.This system is fairly unique to the Akans only.
